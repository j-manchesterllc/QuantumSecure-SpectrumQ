# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We expect participants to uphold a professional and respectful environment.

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Respectful technical debate
- Constructive feedback
- Focus on cryptographic and engineering merit

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment or personal attacks
- Dismissive or abusive language
- Undermining security review processes

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces including issues, pull requests, and discussions.

## Enforcement

Violations may result in warnings or removal from the project.

## Guiding Principle

Security research requires collaboration, rigor, and respect for differing technical perspectives.
